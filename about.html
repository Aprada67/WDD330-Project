About Feast Together

Your smart kitchen assistant for easy, fun, and sustainable cooking

Our Mission

Feast Together helps you transform the ingredients you already have at home into delicious meals. We want to reduce food waste, promote home cooking, and bring joy to your kitchen through intelligent recipe suggestions and fun visuals.

Our Vision

To empower individuals—especially students and busy professionals—to cook creatively with what they have, save money, and discover the satisfaction of preparing meals from scratch.

Core Values

  • Simplicity: Easy-to-use and intuitive design
  • Sustainability: Reduce waste, save ingredients
  • Creativity: Discover new meals and cooking ideas
  • Fun: GIFs and visuals make cooking entertaining

Technologies Used

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6+
  • Spoonacular API for recipe suggestions
  • GIPHY API for fun and engaging visuals
  • Responsive design principles (mobile-first)
  • LocalStorage for favorites
